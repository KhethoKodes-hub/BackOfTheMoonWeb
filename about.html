Our Celestial Story

Discover the journey behind Back of the Moon Guesthouse, where earthly comfort meets cosmic wonder.

Welcome to Our Cosmic Sanctuary

Back of the Moon Guesthouse was born from a dream to create a space where guests could escape the ordinary and connect with something greater. Our founder, Bontle Lehula, envisioned a retreat that would blend the tranquility of nature with the mystery of the cosmos.


Nestled in a serene location that feels worlds away from the hustle of daily life, our guesthouse offers a unique experience where every detail is thoughtfully designed to inspire wonder and relaxation. From our celestial-themed accommodations to our stargazing amenities, we've created an environment that encourages both exploration and introspection.


Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, a solo retreat, or a special celebration, Back of the Moon provides the perfect backdrop for memories that will shine as brightly as the stars above.
