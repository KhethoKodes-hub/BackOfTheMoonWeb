Our Story

Discover the celestial experience that awaits you at Back of the Moon

Where Dreams Meet Reality

Back of the Moon Guesthouse was born from a vision of creating a sanctuary where guests could escape the ordinary and embrace the extraordinary. Named after the mystical "far side of the moon," our establishment represents a place of wonder, tranquility, and hidden beauty.

Our founders believed that true hospitality goes beyond mere accommodation—it's about creating moments of magic, connection, and rejuvenation. Every detail has been carefully crafted to ensure that your stay with us becomes a cherished memory.

Located away from the hustle and bustle, yet accessible to all the attractions you desire, we offer the perfect balance between serenity and convenience.
