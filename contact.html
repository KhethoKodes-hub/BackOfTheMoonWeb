Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 7 days before your scheduled arrival. Cancellations within 7 days may be subject to a fee. Please contact us directly for specific circumstances.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we can arrange airport transfers for an additional fee. Please let us know your flight details when booking, and we'll coordinate your celestial arrival.

Are pets allowed at the guesthouse?

We love animals, but to maintain our tranquil environment, we generally do not allow pets. Service animals are always welcome - please notify us in advance.

Is the guesthouse suitable for children?

Absolutely! We welcome families and can provide extra bedding or cribs upon request. Our stargazing activities are enjoyed by guests of all ages.

What amenities are included?

All stays include access to our stargazing deck, infinity pool, lounge areas, and complimentary breakfast. Room amenities vary by accommodation type.
